# ECB policy calendar

url: https://www.ecb.europa.eu/press/calendars/mgcgc/html/index.en.html

## Good for

- Primary dates for Governing Council monetary-policy meetings.
- Standard publication times for the policy decision and press conference.
- Post-event decision releases, statements, and transcripts.

## Bad at

- The site often labels times as `CET` year-round, so UTC conversion during European summer time requires an explicit Europe/Berlin timezone conversion rather than a fixed UTC+1 assumption.
- Dynamic or localized pages can expose stale "previous/next" metadata; corroborate the meeting date with an independent calendar.
- It does not provide market consensus or expected rate probabilities.
